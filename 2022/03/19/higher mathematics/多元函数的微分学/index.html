二元Taylor

$f(x,y)$在$P_0(x_0,y_0)$的领域内有n+1阶连续偏导数

$f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0+\Delta y)=\sum_{k=0}^n \frac{1}{k !}(\Delta x\frac{\partial x}+\Delta y\frac{\partial}{\partial y})^k f|_{(x_0,y_0)}+R_n$

  • Lagrange余项 $R_n=\frac{1}{(n+1)!}(\Delta x\frac{\partial x}+\Delta y\frac{\partial}{\partial y})^{n+1} f|_{(x_0+\theta \Delta x,y_0+ \theta \Delta y)}$

  • Peano余项 $R_n=o(\rho^n)$ $\rho=\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}$

    只要求n阶连续偏导数

Lagrange中值定理 $f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0+\Delta y)-f(x_0,y_0)=f_x(x_0+\theta \Delta x,y_0+ \theta \Delta y)\Delta x+f_y(x_0+\theta \Delta x,y_0+ \theta \Delta y)\Delta y$

Lagrange取n=0

$\theta \in (0,1)$
